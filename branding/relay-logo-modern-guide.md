# Relay Modern Logo Concepts

Three cleaner concepts built with modern identity best practices.

## Design Principles Applied

1. Monochrome first.
2. No tagline in master lockup.
3. Simple geometry and consistent stroke weight.
4. Works on white and dark backgrounds.
5. Legible at small sizes and app icon scale.

## Concepts

1. relay-concept-a-orbit.svg
- Meaning: handoff loop and continuity.
- Strength: most balanced and premium.
- Risk: circular marks are common in SaaS.

2. relay-concept-b-gate.svg
- Meaning: controlled execution and direction.
- Strength: strongest uniqueness.
- Risk: most abstract; needs brand repetition.

3. relay-concept-c-link.svg
- Meaning: connection and relaying between steps.
- Strength: most literal to the product behavior.
- Risk: can feel generic if overused.

## Additional Concepts

4. relay-concept-d-pivot.svg
- Meaning: multi-step handoff through a central pivot.
- Strength: clear execution pathway and strong icon shape.
- Risk: slightly technical tone.

5. relay-concept-e-track.svg
- Meaning: controlled run lane with directional motion.
- Strength: very clean, excellent small-size legibility.
- Risk: may feel functional over expressive.

6. relay-concept-f-chevron.svg
- Meaning: fast relay and forward movement.
- Strength: bold minimal silhouette.
- Risk: least unique if used without strong wordmark.

7. relay-concept-g-bridge.svg
- Meaning: bridging intent and execution.
- Strength: memorable blocky shape for app icon.
- Risk: slightly abstract at first glance.

8. relay-concept-h-flow.svg
- Meaning: continuous flow with direction markers.
- Strength: dynamic while staying minimal.
- Risk: thin lines need testing at favicon scale.

## Recommendation

Primary: relay-concept-e-track.svg

Secondary icon exploration: relay-concept-a-orbit.svg
