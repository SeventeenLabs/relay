# Relay Logo Shortlist (Non-Generic Direction)

Created from the project strategy: local-first, approval-driven, execution-focused.

## New Lockups

1. relay-logo-sequence.svg
- Idea: three-stage workflow and explicit approval checkpoint.
- Best for: communicating product behavior directly.

2. relay-logo-gateway.svg
- Idea: controlled passage from intent to execution.
- Best for: trust/governance positioning.

3. relay-logo-helix.svg
- Idea: dual flow paths and handoff continuity.
- Best for: premium, less literal brand tone.

4. relay-logo-rune.svg
- Idea: distinctive R mark built as a single stroke language.
- Best for: strongest brand ownership and memorability.

## Why These Are Less Generic

1. Each concept maps to a specific product mechanic, not abstract AI motifs.
2. Monochrome-first geometry for timelessness and system compatibility.
3. Distinct silhouette focus for favicon and app-icon recognition.
4. No gradient, sparkles, or stock SaaS symbol language.
